Questions

Frequently asked

What exactly do I receive?
Digital files only — 40 DXF files for CNC aluminium cutting, 6 STL files for 3D printing reference, plus assembly documentation. You manufacture the connectors yourself and purchase materials locally.
Do I need a building permit in Norway?
No. All FJELL packages are ≤15m² BYA, exempt under PBL §20-4. Most EU countries have similar exemptions for structures under 15-30m².
How long does it take to build?
Approximately 52 hours with 2-4 people — typically one weekend. Assumes CNC-cut connectors ready and materials sourced.
What's the total cost including materials?
Digital files start at €250. Materials cost approximately €7,480-€8,570 for a self-build. Full turnkey ~€12,170-€12,770.
What software do I need?
DXF files open in LibreCAD (free), AutoCAD, FreeCAD, or any CAD software. Send directly to any aluminium CNC fabricator.

Ready to build?

Get professional DXF & STL files. Manufacture connectors locally. Build in a weekend.
