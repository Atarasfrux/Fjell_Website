Questions

Frequently asked

What exactly do I receive when I purchase?
Digital files only — 40 DXF files for CNC aluminium cutting, 6 STL files for 3D printing reference, plus assembly documentation. You manufacture the connectors yourself at any aluminium fabricator and purchase materials locally. No physical materials are shipped.
Do I need a building permit in Norway?
No. All FJELL packages are ≤15m² BYA (bebygd areal), which exempts them from building permit requirements under the Norwegian Planning and Building Act (PBL) §20-4. For other countries, check local regulations — most EU countries have similar exemptions for structures under 15-30m².
How long does it take to build?
Approximately 52 hours of assembly time with 2-4 people — typically one weekend. This assumes you have CNC-cut connectors ready and materials sourced. If you're manufacturing connectors yourself, add time for CNC fabrication.
What's the total cost including materials?
Digital files start at 2,500 NOK. Materials cost approximately 70,000-74,000 NOK total for a self-build (LAND package), depending on your market and choices. That's roughly 85% less than a traditional cabin of similar quality. Full pre-cut kits coming Q2 2026.
Can I use this for an Airbnb or rental?
Absolutely. Many customers use FJELL cabins as rental properties. The compact footprint and Norwegian design are strong selling points. The included material calculator helps you estimate ROI.
Do you offer assembly services?
Assembly services are available in Norway only (coming Q2 2026). Turnkey pricing (materials + assembly) starts from ~250,000 NOK for LAND. Contact us for details and availability in your area.
What software do I need to open the DXF files?
DXF files can be opened in LibreCAD (free), AutoCAD, FreeCAD, or any CAD software that supports the DXF format. You can send them directly to any aluminium CNC fabricator. STL files can be opened in any 3D printing slicer software.
How does FJELL compare to Norske Mikrohus or Modulbygg?
FJELL is fundamentally different — you buy digital files, not a physical cabin. This means 85% cost savings. Even our upcoming full kits will be 40-75% cheaper than competitors because of our optimized connector system and efficient material usage. You trade some convenience for massive savings.
