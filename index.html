FAQ

Questions answered

Do I need a building permit?+
No. Under 15m² BYA falls under Norway's §20-4 exemption. No permit required.
What tools do I need?+
Standard construction tools: circular saw, drill, level, square. All connections are pre-engineered.
How long does it take?+
With pre-cut files, approximately 52 hours of labor for 2–4 experienced builders.
Can I customize dimensions?+
Yes, the FORM package allows custom dimensions while maintaining structural integrity.
What is included in the CNC kit?+
The File + CNC Connector Hardware Kit includes all digital files plus the aluminium connector hardware (Corner Spine™ × 4, sill channels, wall clips, screw jacks) — cut and delivered to Norway. Timber, insulation, and cladding are sourced locally.
